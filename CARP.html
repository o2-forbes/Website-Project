Below is my finished CARP poster alongside my CARP Template.

My Carp Psoster was produced in Adobe InDesign and contains the application of different typographic systems that I learnt during the semester and produced in Adobe Illustrator. I wanted to include a connection between my CARP Project and my Typography Project to convey that I understand how to apply typographic systems to different forms of digital products.

These typographic sysgtems present consist of Grid, within the information blocks, Dilational, surrounding the radial gradient moon, and some aspects of Transitional, seen through the creation of the mountains.
